# Wilson Premier Properties - Brand Guidelines Assets

## Brand Strategy

### Competency
"Settle for... The Extraordinary"
Set your sights on a truly luxurious lake experience with Wilson Premier Properties at Smith Mountain Lake. Expect thoughtful design, luxurious amenities, impeccable service, and meticulous attention to detail.

### Brand Promise
To go beyond the ordinary and create extraordinary luxury rentals and experiences for our guests and community.

### Brand Values
1. **Authentic**: Simple, elegant, and genuine. Never pretentious.
2. **We've Thought of Everything**: Expect luxury and the exceptional at every touchpoint.
3. **Extraordinary**: A step beyond. Delivering the extraordinary.
4. **Humility**: Honored to serve.
5. **Integrity**: Doing the right thing, even when it's hard.

### Brand Archetype
**CREATOR**
- **Strengths**: Creativity, Imagination, Nonlinear thought, Nonconformity, Developed aesthetic.
- **Goal**: To create things and expressions of lasting value.

### Tagline
"Settle for... the Extraordinary"

## Typography

### Headings: Plantin MT Pro
*Serif typeface, elegant and readable.*
- **Usage**: Headers (Light), Medium Headers & Body Copy Accent (Semi-bold).
- **Alternatives**: Libre Baskerville, EB Garamond (if Plantin MT Pro is unavailable).

### Body: Work Sans
*Sans-serif typeface, clean, modern, and versatile.*
- **Usage**: Body copy, captions.
- **Weights**: Light, Regular, Semi-bold.

## Colors

### Primary Palette
- **Linen**: `#ECE9E7` (Backgrounds, calm, inviting)
- **Taupe**: `#C8B6A6` (Adjusted from `#BCA28A` to be more neutral/elegant sand)
- **Rust**: `#9D5F36` (Warmth, earthy, headers/accents)
- **Charcoal**: `#2B2B2B` (Text, stylish dramatic background)

### Secondary Palette
- **Ruby**: `#AD1D23`
- **Navy**: `#202B54`
- **Forest**: `#337D58`

## Logo Usage
- **Clear Space**: Minimum spacing equal to half the height of the icon.
- **Color**: Always use in a single solid color (Charcoal, White, or Brand Colors). 
- **Pairs**: 
    - Charcoal on Linen
    - Linen on Charcoal
    - Charcoal on Taupe
    - Taupe on White
    - Linen on Rust
    - Rust on White
    - White on Ruby/Navy/Forest
